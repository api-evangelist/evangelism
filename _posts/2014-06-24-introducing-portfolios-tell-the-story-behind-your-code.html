Introducing Portfolios: Tell the Story Behind Your Code We’ve had the privilege of watching amazing software projects come to life over the last few years.Whether browsing through competition galleries online or attending hackathon demos in real life, we’re wowed every single day.You’ve probably seen some of our favorite apps and hacks featured in recent newsletters.But we noticed that outside of challenges and hackathons, there wasn’t a place for our community to get inspired, gain exposure, or showcase software they’d made.That’s why we created Portfolios.
